Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2026

Receipt Vault is designed as a local-first, offline receipt organizer. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information.

Information We Do Not Collect

Receipt Vault does not require account registration and does not ask you to connect a bank account, credit card, or financial institution.

The app does not operate a server that receives your receipts, receipt images, expense records, categories, notes, or exported files.

Receipt Data

Receipt images, OCR text, merchant names, dates, totals, taxes, categories, projects, and notes are stored locally in the app’s storage on your device.

You control any export or sharing action. If you export CSV, PDF, or ZIP files, those files are created locally and shared only through the system share sheet or destination you choose.

Camera and Photo Library

Receipt Vault requests camera access so you can scan receipt images. It requests photo library access so you can import existing receipt photos. The app uses these permissions only for receipt capture and import.

Face ID or Device Authentication

If you enable the app lock, Receipt Vault uses Apple’s local device authentication system to unlock the app. The app does not receive or store your biometric data.

OCR Processing

Receipt Vault uses on-device text recognition to extract receipt information. OCR processing is performed locally on your device.

Analytics and Tracking

Receipt Vault does not include third-party advertising SDKs or tracking SDKs. The app does not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies.

Purchases

If in-app purchases are enabled, purchases are processed by Apple. Receipt Vault does not receive your full payment card details.

Data Deletion

You can delete receipt records in the app. You can also remove all app data by deleting the app from your device.

Children’s Privacy

Receipt Vault is not intended for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app changes. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
